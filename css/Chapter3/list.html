Scarmbled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods, Here is a recipe fpr delicious rich Scarmbled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cram in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20sec (as ifyou are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist removefrom the heat
